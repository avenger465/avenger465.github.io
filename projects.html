Studio Run

Game made in a week for an university assignment

Studio Run

Info

A video Game made for a university assignment where I had to produce a video game with a week. I chose to make an endless runner inspired by Temple Run, however, I took a different spin and chose a motion capture studio as the environment to run through. The obstacles and Power-Ups are themed around a motion capture studio with gaps in the floor to increase the challenge. Collecting coins will boost your final score by providing a multiplier to your distance run.

Features
  • Speed increases over time
  • High-Score  saving
  • Power-Ups 
  • Collectable Coins
Tools and Libraries
  • Unreal Engine 5
